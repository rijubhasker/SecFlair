Services

We offer following

Advisory

These services encompass various elements to ensure that identities such as those of employees, customers, partners, or devices—are properly controlled, authenticated, and secured across systems and applications

IGA Implementation

Focus on helping organizations effectively deploy and integrate identity governance solutions within their systems.These services are aimed at ensuring governance of user access to critical systems and data

CIAM implementation

These services are geared toward providing a seamless and secure experience for customers while ensuring compliance with regulations and protecting sensitive data and giving smooth access to customers.

PAM implementation

These services are designed to help organizations secure and manage access to their most critical systems, networks, and data by controlling and monitoring privileged accounts and activities

Managed Services

These services are designed to handle specific aspects or the entirety of an organization's IAM operations, offering proactive maintenance, monitoring, and management of IAM infrastructure

Training

Focus on educating professionals, IT administrators, and stakeholders about various aspects of IAM solutions, technologies, best practices, and implementation methodologies
